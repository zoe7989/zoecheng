Introducing Broker

My Old Days In GDMFX & SynergyFX

The biggest difference between being a salesperson and an introducing broker(IB) was the way to develop customers. Since forex trading is not a mainstream investment market in Taiwan, few people were familiar with it. Therefore, it indirectly caused many challenges to our job.

My method to deal with that obstacle was to target potential customers through social media, as well as collect useful lists so that we could invite them to our weekly events, educate them with the right mindset of investment and knowledge of forex trading.

The role I played in the team gave me a lot of opportunities to practice public speaking, but it wasn't easy at first, especially I was introverted. However, during a series of trials and errors, I've grown more than I expected eventually and managed to maintain a long-term relationship with many customers.
